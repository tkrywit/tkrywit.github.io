Background

Why start a blog?

I don’t read news any more. I’m not sure if this has been a growing trend, or began with the “fake news” era, but I no longer find the majority of mainstream news and opinion pieces compelling. I, like many others, found myself endlessly scrolling through a set of news sites, looking for something to latch on to. Even if I found something that seemed interesting, it usually had a political bend to it, or has a negative spin to it. Sensationalist, scary news sells, I get it. But realistically, the daily outbursts of Donald Trump or the latest cultural outrage spreading virally don’t interest me. I want authentic stories about real people doing real things, and think that the future of news is local.

Instead of mindlessly scrolling through content, I’ve decided to make my own. Hope you’ll join me!
